About Me

I am an O-Level student from Yuhua Secondary School with a strong interest in biochemistry.

I am particularly fascinated by the intricate processes that take place at the molecular level and how they contribute to life and health.

While biochemistry is my primary interest , I remain open-minded and eager to learn about all fields of science. I believe that having a broad foundation across disciplines is essential for developing a well-rounded scientific perspective.

The Common Science programme at Singapore Polytechnic feels like the ideal next step—an exciting launchpad where I can deepen my understanding. challenge myself alongside like- minded peers, and take one step closer to a future shaped by discovery and innovation.

Building Terrariums

To deepen my understanding of the intricate chemical processes occurring in a plant, I challenged myself to build the perfect terrarium where the ecosystem thrives. Placing plants into the soil and watering them may sound simple. However, for the plants to successfully thrive, they require careful planning and accuracy. It also requires a resilient mindset as you would need to go through many failed terrariums before you finally figure out the perfect conditions. I also studied the plant’s optimal conditions to take the best steps in this challenge.

This experience contributed to my interest in biochemistry as I got to understand how the plants respond to different environments and their different chemical processes!
